Data Discloser Policy

Short Description#

A Data Discloser Policy is a digital policy that enables an operational Data Discloser component to function according to the rules of its Policy Governor.

Such a policy includes e.g. the kinds of data (and meta-data) that may be disclosed, the conditions that need to be satisfied for actually disclosing such kinds of data, any meta-data (assurances) that maybe added to data being disclosed, etc.

Purpose#

The purpose of a Data Discloser Policy is to enable the creation of (technical) components that implement the generic data discloser functionality that will subsequently use such policies to guide their behaviour.

Criteria#

A Data Discloser Policy is a digital policy that enables an operational Data Discloser component to function according to the rules, working-instructions and other guidance of its Policy Governor.
